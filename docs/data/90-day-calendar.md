# 90-Day Workout Calendar

## Overview
This 90-day program is structured in three 30-day phases, with each phase building upon the previous one.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)
**Focus:** Building base strength and establishing routine

### Week 1 (Days 1-7)
- Day 1: Upper Body Strength
- Day 2: Lower Body Strength  
- Day 3: Yoga (30 min)
- Day 4: Core Strength
- Day 5: Full Body Strength
- Day 6: Plyometric
- Day 7: Rest

### Week 2 (Days 8-14)

- Day 9: Lower Body Strength
- Day 10: Yoga (30 min)
- Day 11: Core Strength

- Day 13: Plyometric
- Day 14: Rest

### Week 3 (Days 15-21)

- Day 20: Plyometric
