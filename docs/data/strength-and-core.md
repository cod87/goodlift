# Strength and Core Workout Templates

## Upper Body Strength
**Equipment:** Barbell, Dumbbells, Pull-up Bar
**Duration:** 45-60 minutes
**Sets/Reps:** 4 sets x 8-12 reps

### Exercises
1. Barbell Bench Press
2. Pull-Ups (or Assisted Pull-Ups)
3. Overhead Press
4. Barbell Bent-Over Row
5. Dips (or Bench Dips)
6. Bicep Curls
7. Tricep Extensions

## Upper Body Power
**Equipment:** Barbell, Dumbbells, Pull-up Bar
**Duration:** 45-60 minutes
**Sets/Reps:** 5 sets x 5-8 reps (heavier weight)

### Exercises
1. Barbell Bench Press (heavy)
2. Weighted Pull-Ups
3. Overhead Press (heavy)
4. Barbell Bent-Over Row (heavy)
5. Weighted Dips
6. Barbell Curls

## Lower Body Strength
**Equipment:** Barbell, Dumbbells

### Exercises

5. Leg Curl
6. Calf Raises

## Lower Body Power
**Equipment:** Barbell, Dumbbells

## Full Body Strength
**Equipment:** Barbell, Dumbbells, Pull-up Bar
**Duration:** 60-75 minutes
**Sets/Reps:** 4 sets x 8-12 reps

### Exercises
1. Deadlift
2. Barbell Bench Press
3. Back Squat
4. Pull-Ups
5. Overhead Press
6. Barbell Bent-Over Row
7. Core work (Planks, Russian Twists)

**Equipment:** Barbell, Dumbbells, Pull-up Bar
**Duration:** 60-75 minutes

### Exercises
1. Deadlift (heavy)
2. Barbell Bench Press (heavy)
3. Back Squat (heavy)

## Core Strength
**Equipment:** Bodyweight, Optional: Medicine Ball, Cable Machine
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes
**Sets/Reps:** 3-4 sets x 12-20 reps or 30-60 sec holds

### Exercises
1. Planks (Front, Side)
2. Russian Twists
3. Leg Raises (Hanging or Lying)
4. Cable Crunches (if available)
5. Dead Bug
6. Bird Dog
7. Mountain Climbers
8. Bicycle Crunches
9. Hollow Body Hold
10. Ab Wheel Rollouts (if available)

## Exercise Notes
- **Light:** 60-70% of max weight, focus on form
- **Moderate:** 70-80% of max weight, steady pace
- **Heavy/Power:** 80-90% of max weight, explosive movement
- Always warm up with 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching
- Rest 60-90 seconds between sets for strength
- Rest 90-120 seconds between sets for power
