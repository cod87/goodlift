PLYOMETRIC TRAINING GUIDE

## Plyometric Session Structure

Each plyometric session follows this pattern:
- Warm-up: 5 minutes dynamic stretching
- Main Circuit: 4 rounds of 6 exercises
- Work: 40 seconds per exercise
- Rest: 20 seconds between exercises
- Rest between rounds: 2 minutes

## Exercise Pool

### Lower Body Plyo
- Box Jumps (use 20-24 inch box)
- Jump Squats (bodyweight)
- Lateral Bounds (10 yards each direction)
- Single Leg Hops (alternate legs each round)
- Tuck Jumps (maximum height)
- Broad Jumps (focus on distance)

### Upper Body Plyo
- Clapping Push-Ups
- Medicine Ball Slams (use 10-15 lb ball)
- Plyo Push-Ups (explosive)

### Full Body
- Burpees (full range)
- Mountain Climbers (fast pace)
- Jumping Lunges (alternate legs)

## Progression
Week 1-2: 3 rounds, 30 sec work / 30 sec rest
Week 3-4: 3 rounds, 40 sec work / 20 sec rest
Week 5+: 4 rounds, 40 sec work / 20 sec rest

## Safety Notes
- Always land softly with knees slightly bent
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Stop if experiencing joint pain
